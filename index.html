Akan Name Generator

Find out your Akan name with this handy generator app, simply add your date of birth.

How it works

In Ghana's Akan culture, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day of the week they were born on. The Akan Name Generator works by taking in your date of birth and your gender. It uses this information to first figure out the day of your birth, and then to assign your Akan name on that basis. Have fun naming your friends and family in the Akan tradition using this resource!
